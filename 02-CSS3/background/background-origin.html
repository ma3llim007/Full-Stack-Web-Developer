The Background-origin property specifies where the background image is positioned. we can set this property to border-box, padding-box, content-box.

  1. Border-Box :-The Background image starts from the upper left corner of the border
  2. Padding-Box :-The Background Image starts from the upppr left corner of the paddign edge. its default value
  3. Context-Box :-The Background Image starts from the upppr left corner of the content
