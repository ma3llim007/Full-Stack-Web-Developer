Word Break

the word-break property specifies line breaking rules for non-CJK scripts.CJK script are chinese, japanese and korean("CJK") scripts. we can set this property to normal, break-all or keep all

  1. NORMAL :- default value. break word according to their usual rules
  2. BREAK-ALL :- lines may break between any two letters
  3. KEEP-ALL :- breaks are prohibited between paris of letters
